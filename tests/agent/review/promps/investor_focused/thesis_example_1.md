Aurora Seed Partners

Core belief
The most capital-efficient and scalable outcomes in technology are built through pure software businesses. We believe enduring companies are created by abstracting complexity away from the physical world, not by engaging with it directly. At the seed stage, simplicity, speed of iteration, and low capital requirements are essential.

Aurora Seed Partners is a software-only seed fund. We focus exclusively on companies where value is created through code, workflow design, and distribution, without dependence on physical products, hardware development, or operational infrastructure.

Focus
Aurora Seed Partners invests at the pre-seed and seed stages in pure software companies. Our investments are limited to businesses where the product can be built, shipped, iterated, and scaled without designing, manufacturing, or deploying physical systems.

We invest in
• SaaS products and software platforms
• Developer tools, productivity software, and workflow automation
• Data, analytics, and application-layer software
• Consumer and enterprise software with digital-only delivery

We do not invest in
• Hardware, robotics, drones, or autonomous systems
• Companies requiring manufacturing, supply chains, or physical deployment
• Businesses where software is coupled to proprietary devices
• Capital-intensive or asset-heavy models

What we look for
We back founders who move quickly, ship early, and learn directly from users. Strong teams build simple products, demonstrate early engagement, and show a clear path to software-driven growth.

We value
• Clear ICP and use case
• Short feedback loops
• Low burn and high learning velocity
• Products that can scale without operational complexity

Stage and approach
Aurora Seed Partners invests early and prefers businesses that can reach meaningful usage with minimal capital. We support founders through early product development and initial go-to-market learning, but we avoid models that require long development timelines or external dependencies before value is proven.

Our conviction
Pure software remains the most powerful and repeatable vehicle for early-stage value creation. Aurora Seed Partners exists to back founders building focused, software-only businesses that can scale efficiently and independently of the physical world.
