Skyline Seed Partners

Core belief
Durable technology companies are built by creating flexible software platforms that adapt as customer needs evolve. We believe the strongest businesses use software to abstract complexity, improve coordination, and enable better decision-making across a range of environments and use cases.

Focus
Early-stage teams building software-centric platforms that support complex workflows and multi-step processes. We are interested in products that improve visibility, coordination, and execution for users operating in dynamic or distributed settings.

We prioritize
• Software platforms designed for adaptability rather than single-purpose use
• Products that integrate cleanly into existing workflows or systems
• Teams that iterate closely with users and refine based on feedback
• Businesses with the potential to serve multiple industries or customer types

Avoid
Hardware-led differentiation, manufacturing-dependent business models, and products that require deep operational complexity before value can be demonstrated.

Stage
Seed and Series A, where early product and positioning decisions create leverage while maintaining flexibility as the company discovers its strongest use cases.
