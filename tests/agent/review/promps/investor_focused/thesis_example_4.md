Skyline Growth Capital

Core belief
Defining platforms in autonomy and intelligent systems are built by tightly coupling software and hardware into operationally proven products. Enduring leaders emerge once these systems demonstrate reliability at scale, survive real-world deployment, and become embedded in mission-critical workflows.

We believe the winners in this category are not experiments or prototypes, but companies that have already crossed the threshold from technical promise to operational necessity.

Focus
Growth-stage companies building integrated software and hardware platforms for autonomous or semi-autonomous systems. We invest in businesses where core architecture is established, deployments are active, and software leverage compounds across fleets, customers, or geographies.

We prioritize
• Proven platforms with production deployments
• Software-defined systems operating on proprietary or tightly integrated hardware
• Demonstrated customer usage in real operational environments
• Clear expansion paths through additional missions, customers, or scale

Avoid
Pre-product companies, early prototypes, and teams still validating core technical feasibility or initial market demand.

Stage
Series B and later, where product-market fit is established, unit economics are visible, and the focus shifts from proving capability to scaling deployment, revenue, and organizational maturity.
